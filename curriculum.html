Duration: 6 Weeks

Format: Blended – Theoretical Sessions + Hands-on Assignments + Capstone Project

  1. Introduction to Financial Management
    • Objective of finance function
    • Types of financial decisions (investment, financing, dividend)
    • Overview of financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  2. Accounting Principles & Standards
    • GAAP & IFRS basics
    • Double-entry system
    • Accrual vs. Cash accounting
  3. Understanding Corporate Finance
    • Capital structure and cost of capital
    • Types of funding: Equity, Debt, Mezzanine
  4. Practical: Journal Entries and Financial Statements (Tally or Zoho Books)

Week 2: Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)

    • Fixed vs. flexible budgets

  2. Variance Analysis
    • Budget vs. actual analysis
    • Understanding KPIs and variance drivers
  3. Excel for Finance (Level 1)
    • Basic formulas: SUM, IF, VLOOKUP, PIVOT Tables
    • Financial statement modeling basics
  4. Practical: Budget creation & variance analysis in Excel

Week 3: Investment & Risk Management

  1. Time Value of Money
    • Present and future value
    • Annuities and perpetuities
  2. Investment Appraisal Techniques
    • NPV, IRR, Payback Period, Profitability Index
  3. Introduction to Risk Management
    • Types of financial risk (market, credit, liquidity)
    • Risk mitigation strategies
  4. Practical: NPV & IRR calculation using Excel or Google Sheets

Week 4: Taxation, Compliance & Automation

  1. Direct & Indirect Taxes (India-focused or localised)
    • GST basics, TDS, corporate tax overview
  2. Financial Compliance
    • Statutory compliance calendar
    • Audit checklists and internal control basics
  3. AI Tools in Finance
    • ChatGPT for financial analysis, ratio explanation, automation

  4. Practical: GST invoice creation + AI-assisted financial ratio interpretation

Week 5: Reporting, Dashboards & ERP Tools

  1. Financial Reporting Standards
    • MIS Reports, Board Reports
    • Monthly/quarterly closing reports
  2. Power BI / Google Data Studio for Finance
    • Creating dashboards from Excel data
    • Visualizing KPIs: Revenue, Expenses, Margins
  3. ERP & Accounting Software
    • Intro to QuickBooks, Tally Prime, Zoho Books
  4. Practical: Build a finance dashboard + basic report using Power BI or Google Sheets

Week 6: Capstone Project & Career Readiness

  1. Capstone Project
    • End-to-end financial case: Budgeting, investment decision, variance analysis, and dashboard reporting (Team or individual submission)
  2. Resume Building for Finance Roles
    • Keywords, achievements, tools to highlight
  3. Mock Interviews & Role Plays
    • Common finance internship questions
    • Situational questions & practical case-based discussions
  4. Final Evaluation & Certification
    • Project presentation
    • Performance review & feedback
