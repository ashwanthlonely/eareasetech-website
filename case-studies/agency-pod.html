Drop Servicing Pod for a Remote Agency

How we helped a two-person agency duo scale to 18 concurrent projects, grow revenue by 41%, and keep client churn below 2% — all without adding permanent headcount.

The Challenge

A bootstrapped, two-person digital agency had hit a ceiling. They were managing 6 client projects simultaneously — web development, SEO, and social media — and every new contract meant personal burnout. They couldn't afford to hire, but turning down work meant capping revenue.

They needed a way to scale delivery capacity without the overhead of full-time employees, and without compromising the quality their clients expected.

Our Approach

We onboarded them onto our Calm Ops Retainer, which bundles drop servicing execution with QA governance and team wellbeing:

  • White-label production pod: A dedicated team of 4 specialists (frontend, SEO, paid ads, content) operating under the agency's brand.
  • Proposal lab: We co-authored client proposals and SOWs, bringing our delivery expertise into their sales process.
  • QA governance: Every deliverable passed through a two-stage review — technical QA and client-readiness audit — before handoff.
  • Wellness stipends: Monthly virtual town halls, "no-meeting Fridays" calendar blocks, and wellness kits (journaling guides, playlists, ergonomic tips) for the founders.
  • Client rituals: We introduced weekly 15-minute client syncs replacing lengthy status emails, improving transparency and trust.

The Results

Over the first 6 months of the retainer:

  • Project capacity tripled: 6 → 18 concurrent projects
  • Revenue grew by 41% without adding a single full-time hire
  • Client churn dropped to <2% (from 12% pre-engagement)
  • Founders reported a 55% reduction in personal stress levels
  • Average client NPS rose from 34 to 72
