Who We Are

EarEase-Tech is an initiative to create jobs, reduce stress in tech careers, and upskill future professionals with real-world training, drop servicing projects, and mental wellness activities like weekend rides, career counseling, and listening sessions.

Our Services

IT Drop Servicing

Get your projects done by trained professionals and fresh talents guided by senior engineers.

Training & Mentoring

Affordable AI, MERN stack, and Data Science mentoring to empower students and jobseekers.

Mental Wellness Activities

We offer weekend trips, creative breaks, and a low-stress work model for better mental health.

Listening Sessions

Personalized sessions where individuals can talk, vent, and feel heard in a safe, non-judgmental environment.

Career Counselling

Guided paths for students and professionals with personalized advice on learning, career growth, and transition.
